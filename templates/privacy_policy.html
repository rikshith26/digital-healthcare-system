Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs how HealthLab AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and processes your information when you use our platform, application, and website. We respect your privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws to ensure your information is secure. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies you when you register, use our services, or interact with our platform. This includes:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Demographic Data: Date of birth, gender, and nationality.
  • Health Information: Medical records, history, appointment details, prescriptions, diagnostic data, and interactions with our AI health assistant.
  • Financial Information: Billing details required to process transactions securely.
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, browsing history on our platform, and cookies to improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used to provide and enhance our healthcare services:

  • To facilitate doctor consultations, lab tests, and medical deliveries.
  • To maintain your unified electronic health records for seamless care.
  • To personalize our AI assistant's insights and recommendations.
  • To process payments and manage billing.
  • To communicate updates, appointment reminders, and important security notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or medical data to third parties. Information is only shared on a strict need-to-know basis with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Doctors, technicians, and pharmacies involved directly in your care.
  • Service Partners: Secure payment gateways, cloud hosting providers, and communication tools bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Law Enforcement: When legally required by authorities to comply with applicable laws.

5. Data Security

We implement robust, industry-standard technical and administrative safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. While we strive for maximum security, no digital platform can guarantee absolute invulnerability, and users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements. If you choose to delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed from our active systems, except where retention is legally mandated for medical or financial compliance.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, review, and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing (which may limit your ability to use certain services).

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, to exercise your rights, or to report a grievance, please reach out to our dedicated support team at privacy@healthlabai.com.
